## Context

**Client:** Developer services company, ~50 employees, ~1,400 repos, ~300 GitLab groups, multi-model AI tooling, mostly VS Code.

**What we're selling:**
1. **Phase 1 — Shared Memory:** Connect 50 devs to shared AI memory, GitLab SSO, permission-aware
2. **Phase 2 — Knowledge Engineering:** Scan 1,400 repos, extract conventions, create living knowledge packs
3. **Phase 3 — AI Development Team:** Three AI roles: Chief of Staff, Insight Agent, Onboarding Companion

**Key selling points:**
- Pays for itself in < 3 months (20 min/day saved per dev = 28K EUR/mo recovered)
- Generic AI tools can't do this — requires organizational memory
- Self-hosted, data never leaves their infra
- Design partnership = 20% discount + roadmap influence
- We use Claude Code extensively — this is how we deliver fast

**Pricing summary:**
- Project delivery: ~40K EUR (after 20% design partner discount)
- Monthly support: 2,650 EUR/mo (starts after Phase 1)
- First-year total: ~65K EUR

**Three AI roles to remember:**
- **AI Chief of Staff** — knows everything happening across the org, answers any question
- **Insight Agent** — proactively surfaces patterns and problems you didn't ask about
- **Onboarding Companion** — new hire to contributor in days, not months

**Presentation is in Slovenian.** The written proposal and cost breakdown are in English.
